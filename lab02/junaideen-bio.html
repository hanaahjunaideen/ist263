Hanaah Junaideen's Biography

Hanaah was born in Greenwich, Connecticut as a triplet. She lived in Connecticut with my family until she was 9, and then they moved to Potomac, Maryland, which is where she still live today.

When the college application process was starting during her junior year of high school, Hanaah never expected to want to go to Syracuse. Because she went to a really small Episcopalian high school, she thought she wanted to go to a college with a similar style. This all changed, however, when she did a virtual campus tour of Syracuse. Hanaah immediately fell in love with the school, applied Early Decision, and have been loving her time here ever since.

Hanaah Junaideen is a junior majoring in Policy Studies and minoring in Information Technology Management. She is interested in researching statistics related to crime and healthcare. She have a strong skillset in all of Microsoft suite, specifically Excel.
